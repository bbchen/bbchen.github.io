About

I am an Earth Scientist from Honolulu, Hawaii, USA. I am passionate about science, technology, art, literature, and nature. Through this platform, I am sharing my thoughts, ideas, tips, and cool stuff to you and other readers.

This website is generated with Hugo, hosted on GitHub Pages, and open sourced on GitHub. Articles are licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution license.

These are some of the applications, websites, and technologies that I use on a regular basis:

  • Sublime Text – source code editor with a wide variety of plugins available.
  • iAWriter – minimal but powerful markdown editor. I use it for my daily writings.
  • GitHub – online Git-based project hosting
  • Omnifocus – Mac application adopting the GTD productivity principals.
  • Zen Habits – Zen Habits is about finding simplicity and mindfulness in the daily chaos of our lives. It’s about clearing the clutter so we can focus on what’s important, create something amazing, find happiness.
